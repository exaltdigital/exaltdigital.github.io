About SEO
What actually is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the process of improving your website so that it appears more often — and higher — in Google search results.

When someone searches for a product, service, or topic related to your business, Google decides which websites to show. SEO is the work that helps your site be one of them.

How long does it take to see results?

Honestly — it depends. Most websites start to see meaningful movement in search visibility after 3–6 months of consistent work. Some see early changes sooner.

SEO is not instant. It builds over time, and the sites that stay the course see the best long-term outcomes.

Can you guarantee I'll rank #1 on Google?

No — and anyone who does is not being straight with you. Google's algorithm has hundreds of factors and changes constantly. What we can guarantee is consistent, honest work each month that improves how your site appears in search over time.

Is SEO better than Google Ads?

They serve different purposes. Google Ads put you in front of people immediately — but stop the moment you stop paying. SEO builds over time and keeps working without ongoing ad spend.

We offer both. Monthly SEO plans for long-term growth, and one-time Google Ads campaigns if you want a short-term traffic boost while your SEO builds up.

About Our Service
What do you actually do each month?

It depends on the plan, but typically: fixing technical issues that prevent Google from reading your site properly, improving page titles and structure, expanding the search terms your site can appear for, and making ongoing refinements based on what's working.

Every month you receive a plain English summary of what was done and what changed.

Do you write content for my site?

No — our service focuses on the technical and structural SEO improvements that help Google understand and rank your existing pages. We don't write blog posts or articles as part of our plans.

How many websites do you work on?

Exalt Digital is a solo operation. We deliberately keep the client list small so every site gets genuine attention — not a template approach. If we're at capacity, we'll let you know.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just your website URL. After sign-up we'll review your site and reach out if we need anything else. You don't need to prepare anything in advance.

What's the difference between the SEO plans and a Google Ads campaign?

The monthly SEO plans are ongoing — we work on your site each month to improve your visibility in regular (non-ad) search results. This is a long-term investment that compounds.

The one-time Google Ads campaign is a single payment that puts your site in front of searchers via paid ads immediately. It's a short-term boost — great to run while your SEO is building.
