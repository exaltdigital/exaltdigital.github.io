Why SEO takes time — and why it's worth it

A few honest things you should know before getting started.

🔎

Google has to be able to read your site first

Before you can appear in search results, Google needs to understand your pages. We fix the technical issues that get in the way — things most people never even know exist.

🎯

Your site needs to match what people actually search

We look at the real words and phrases people type into Google, then make sure your pages are aligned with those searches — so Google has a reason to show your site.

📈

Every improvement builds on the last one

Unlike ads, SEO work sticks around. Each month we build on what was done before. The effect compounds — after 6 months, your site is noticeably more visible than it was on day one.

🕑

Results usually take 3–6 months — and that's normal

We'll be straight with you: SEO is not fast. But the sites that stay consistent are the ones that end up being found by far more people. We do the work every month so you don't have to think about it.
